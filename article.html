Honor

Honor is the measure of Arthur's moral standing based on your actions. Honor will increase with actions that show kindness, such as donating to camp, assisting strangers that are injured or stranded in the wild, turning down duels, ect. Actions that will decrease honor include stealing, murdering, antagonizing people, and other unpleasant actions. Honor affects how other characters in the game treat Arthur, and how much shopkeepers charge for items.

Drinks

Alcohol flows freely throughout the wild west. Ride up to one of the many saloons and order yourself a beer or shot of whiskey. You can also find bottles of bourbon, rum, and gin in stores or camps around the land. Drinking will refill your Dead Eye core, but drinking too much will make Arthur drunk. If you continue drinking, Arthur will become wasted and eventually black out.

Dead Eye

Dead Eye allows Arthur to focus when he's shooting. When Dead Eye is active, time slows down so Arthur can plan out his shots effectively. Using Dead Eye will drain your Dead Eye core, which can be replenished by drinking alcohol, smoking cigars or cigarrettes, drinking coffee, or eating foods flavored with thyme. As you progress through the story, Arthur's abilities in Dead Eye will be upgraded.

Activities

Arthur can partake in various activities across the frontier. You can stop by any river or lake and spend some time fishing. Various table top games can be found at saloons or around camp, such as dominoes, blackjack, poker, and five finger fillet. Sometimes Arthur may happen across a gunslinger who will challenge Arthur to a sharpshooting competition.
